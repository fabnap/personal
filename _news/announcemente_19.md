New ArXiv preprint out! "Lund Plane to Bloch (LP2B) Encoding for Object and Polarization Tagging with Quantum Jet Substructure" is now available. This paper introduces a novel encoding method for jet substructure analysis in high-energy physics, leveraging quantum computing techniques. The LP2B encoding scheme is designed to enhance the performance of quantum algorithms in identifying and classifying particles based on their jet substructure and polarization characteristics. We demonstrate its effectiveness in a series of benchmark tests. Check it out here: [link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.18613)
